6. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When we transfer data internationally, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with EU-recognized adequate protection
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved contracts with data processors
  • Binding Corporate Rules: Internal data protection policies for multinational transfers
  • Encryption: Data encryption during transfer and storage

You have the right to request information about the safeguards used for international transfers of your data.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data and information about how it's processed.

How: Profile → Download My Data
Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

How: Profile → Edit Profile
Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").

How: Profile → Delete Account
Right to Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format for transfer to another service.

How: Profile → Download My Data
Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

How: Privacy Settings
Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

How: Contact DPO
Response Time

We will respond to your rights requests within 30 days (or 60 days for complex requests). Requests are generally processed free of charge.

10. Data Security

10.1 Technical Safeguards
  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Password Security: bcrypt hashing with salt
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, monitoring
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and vulnerability assessments
10.2 Organizational Measures
  • Staff Training: Regular privacy and security training
  • Access Limitation: Principle of least privilege
  • Incident Response: Data breach response procedures
  • Audit Logs: Comprehensive logging and monitoring
  • Third-Party Vetting: Security assessment of vendors
10.3 Data Breach Notification

In case of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify supervisory authorities within 72 hours (where required)
  • Inform affected users without undue delay
  • Provide clear information about the breach and mitigation steps
  • Offer credit monitoring or other protection services if appropriate

11. Children's Privacy

WiseNews is designed for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent.

11.1 Age Verification
  • Users must provide their date of birth during registration
  • Accounts for users under 16 require parental consent
  • We may request additional verification for young users
11.2 Parental Rights

Parents/guardians of users under 18 can:

  • Request information about data collected from their child
  • Request correction or deletion of their child's data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Contact us at parents@wisenews.com

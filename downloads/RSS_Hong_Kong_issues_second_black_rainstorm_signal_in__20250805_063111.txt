Hong Kong’s weather forecaster issues a black rainstorm signal for a second time in six hours, with heavy showers flooding different areas and bringing the city to a standstill on Tuesday.
The Hong Kong Observatory says the highest-level rainstorm warning will be kept in force until at least 3pm. So far, 101 flights are delayed, two cancelled and two diverted to other airports.

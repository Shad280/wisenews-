The Group of 20 should consider improving its debt restructuring framework for poor nations, the former vice president of the African Development Bank said.
The G-20 needs to “think about debt restructuring frameworks and how to make them work,” Swazi Tshabalala told a conference in Johannesburg on Wednesday discussing the cost of capital, adding that the bloc needs to commit to firm timelines for implementation.

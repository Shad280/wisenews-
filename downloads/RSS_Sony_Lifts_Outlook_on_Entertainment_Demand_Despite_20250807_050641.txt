Tech Earnings:
Sony Group Corp. shares surged the most in four months after the Japanese company lifted its earnings forecast on growth in its entertainment divisions, easing investor fears about possible US tariffs on chips.
Momentum in Sony’s music and games segments bolstered the Tokyo-based company’s outlook, the company said Thursday. The number of monthly active users on its PlayStation platform rose 6% from a year ago, alongside total hours of gameplay. That’s while its music segment logged a 5% revenue increase despite a hefty forex impact.

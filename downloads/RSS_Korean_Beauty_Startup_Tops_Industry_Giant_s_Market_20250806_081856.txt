A South Korean company behind popular home-beauty devices surpassed an industry leader’s market value, as its stock hit a record after strong second-quarter earnings.
Shares of APR Corp. soared 11% on Wednesday. The stock has more than quadrupled this year, fueled by strong overseas demand for its cosmetics that pushed its market value to nearly 8 trillion won ($5.8 billion). It overtook the market capitalization of the nation’s No. 1 cosmetics brand Amorepacific Corp., and is closing in on Japan’s Shiseido Co.

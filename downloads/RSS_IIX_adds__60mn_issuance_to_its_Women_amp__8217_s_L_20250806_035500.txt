Singapore-based Impact Investment Exchange (IIX) closed a $60 million Women’s Livelihood Bond (WLB) issuance at the end of July. The notes consist of a $52.8 million four-year, 5.88% senior bond, as well as $7.2 million of subordinated debt, a de-risking facility.
